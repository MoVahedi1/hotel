Booking & Reservations

How do I make a hotel booking?

Making a booking is simple: 1) Search for your desired hotel and dates, 2) Select your preferred room, 3) Enter guest details, 4) Complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking through your account dashboard. Modification and cancellation policies vary by hotel and rate type. Most bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund.

How do I make a group booking?

For group bookings (10+ rooms), please contact our group reservations team at groups@luxuryhotels.com or call 1-800-LUXURY-1. We offer special rates and dedicated support for group bookings.

I haven't received my booking confirmation. What should I do?

First, check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find it, contact our support team with your name and booking details. We can resend the confirmation and provide your booking reference number.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for corporate bookings. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. Our payment gateway is PCI DSS compliant, and we never store your complete credit card information on our servers.

What currency are prices displayed in?

Prices are displayed in USD by default, but you can change your preferred currency in your account settings. The final charge will be in your local currency at the current exchange rate.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. The price you see during booking includes all taxes and fees. Some hotels may charge additional fees for services like parking or resort fees, which are clearly disclosed during booking.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

Click "Register" in the top navigation, fill in your details, and verify your email address. Account creation is free and takes less than 2 minutes. Members enjoy exclusive deals and faster checkout.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.

What are the benefits of creating an account?

Account holders enjoy: exclusive member rates, faster checkout, booking history management, saved preferences, loyalty points, and personalized recommendations based on your travel history.

Hotels & Amenities

What amenities are typically included?

Amenities vary by hotel but commonly include: WiFi, complimentary breakfast, fitness center, swimming pool, spa services, room service, concierge, and business center. Check individual hotel pages for specific amenities.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and may incur additional charges.

Are pets allowed?

Pet policies vary by hotel. Some welcome pets with additional fees, while others don't allow pets. Filter your search by "Pet Friendly" or check the hotel's pet policy before booking.
