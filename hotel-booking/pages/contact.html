Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

Simply search for your desired hotel, select your dates and room preferences, and click "Book Now". Follow the checkout process to confirm your reservation with our secure payment system.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by hotel. Most bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. Check the specific hotel's policy during booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for select corporate bookings.

How can I modify my booking?

Log into your account and go to "My Bookings". Select the booking you wish to modify and follow the prompts. Changes are subject to availability and hotel policies.
